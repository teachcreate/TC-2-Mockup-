About Sarah

With over 15 years of classroom experience, I've dedicated my career to making learning engaging and accessible for all students. My journey began in a small rural school where I discovered the power of hands-on, interactive learning.

I specialize in creating educational tools that bridge the gap between traditional teaching methods and modern technology. My tools are classroom-tested and designed with real student needs in mind.

When I'm not creating new educational resources, you'll find me mentoring new teachers, presenting at education conferences, or spending time with my own two children who are my greatest inspiration.
